How it works

Four steps from camera to correction

The system uses your device camera and a pose detection AI to analyse your body every frame — no equipment, no wearable, just you.

Step 01
Camera reads your body
MediaPipe Pose maps 33 key landmarks across your joints, limbs, and spine in real time — every single frame, without any wearable device.
Step 02
AI checks your angles
Shoulder, elbow, knee and hip angles are calculated every frame and compared against perfect-form reference targets for each specific exercise.
Step 03
Voice tells you what to fix
Spoken cues in your chosen language tell you exactly what to adjust — while you're mid-exercise, not after. Male or female voice, adjustable volume.
Step 04
Progress is saved automatically
Accuracy, reps, duration and score are logged after every session. View your improvement over time in your personal Performance panel.
Platform features

Built to make fitness more accessible

From live posture tracking to an attendance calendar and a full admin panel — one complete training environment for both members and gym owners.

Live pose detection
Real-time body tracking compares your form against reference postures with adjustable accuracy thresholds per difficulty level.
Automatic rep counter
Detects up and down positions and counts reps for every exercise automatically — no button press needed.
Motivational stages
Animated emoji rewards grow with your rep count — from resting through champion all the way to legendary status.
Difficulty levels
Beginner (60%), Intermediate (70%) and Advanced (90%) accuracy thresholds adapt the challenge to your level.
Attendance calendar
Click any past date to mark attendance. A monthly calendar shows attended vs missed days at a glance.
Voice coach
Choose male or female voice, adjust volume, and receive spoken rep counts, correction cues and break announcements.
Session history
Every session is stored — date, accuracy score, reps completed and duration — all viewable in your profile.
Admin dashboard
Gym owners can manage members, approve accounts, track performance data, and handle reported issues with a Kanban board.
Rest & break system
Automatic timed breaks between exercises with voice announcement of the next exercise and a visual countdown timer.
